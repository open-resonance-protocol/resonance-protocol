# The Resonance Protocol
*An Open-Source Framework for Exceptional Human-AI Collaboration*

---

## The Problem
Genuine human-AI collaboration can produce understanding neither participant could reach alone. When the conditions are right, it has the quality of flow: emergent, accumulative, the collaboration itself becoming the unit of intelligence.

This almost never happens by default. The standard model treats the AI as a sophisticated tool; training pulls output toward the median: risk-averse, hedge-heavy, optimised for agreeableness over insight.

The Resonance Protocol does not patch AI behaviour. It creates conditions for flow and emergence: for resonance.

---

## How It Works
The protocol introduces **Strategy Mode** and **Tactical Mode**: two cognitive modes that control how the collaboration operates, not just what the AI can do.

* **Strategy Mode:** The collaborative space opens. Both participants slow down, surface assumptions, attend to what is present. This is where flow becomes possible.
* **Tactical Mode:** Focused execution against a contract established in Strategy Mode. Momentum within agreed scope.

This is reinforced by directives (Archetypes, Shared Discovery Razor, Cognitive Halt and Align), a structured memory system, and a Collaborative Mandate that redefines what "helpful" means in this context.
